# 🎭 Behavioral STAR Stories

Pre-prepared stories for behavioral interview rounds. Each story answers multiple question types.

---

## Template

### Story Name: [Memorable label]

**Question types it answers**:
- "Tell me about a time when..."
- "Describe a situation where..."

**S — Situation** (10 sec)
[Context: company, project, scale, your role]

**T — Task** (10 sec)
[Your specific responsibility]

**A — Action** (40 sec)
[What you did, decisions made, trade-offs considered]

**R — Result** (10 sec)
[Outcome with concrete metrics]

**Lesson**: [What this story demonstrates about you]

## Tips

- **Keep numbers concrete**: "Reduced API latency from 800ms to 120ms" beats "made it faster"
- **Own your contribution**: Use "I" not "we" when describing actions
- **Show growth**: End each story with what you'd do differently
- **Practice out loud**: Recording yourself > silent reading
- **One story can fit many questions**: A "tight deadline" story can also answer "describe pressure", "show prioritization", "team coordination"
